To refine (the "hybrid" step): open either PNG in Piskel / Aseprite / Photopea, clean up pixels, save over the same file, and refresh this page.
To re-roll the auto-convert with a different look: python3 v2/art/convert.py v2/art/plum_src.png v2/art/plum.png --h 38 --colors 12 (try --colors 8–24).
Walk & cheer are whole-sprite motion for now (hop-bob + a jump). Per-leg animation = draw 2–3 frames in the editor; then I wire them to cycle.
